Briefly explain the vulnerability and why the secure version prevents the vulnerability.

When the cookie configuration in Insecure.js is set to false. This means that the cookies 
are vulnerable to an attack because everyone can have access to the client side scripts.
In accordance with the above that means anyone with access to the cookies can be an authenticated user.
Meanwhile secure prevents this by denying access to client side scripts with means attackers
cannot impersonate a logged in user
